How to Play

  • Click on the Start button.
  • Follow and repeat the sequence of lights and sounds that Simon plays. As the game progresses, the sequence gets longer and harder.
  • Simon lights up one colored button and plays a corresponding tone.
  • Your Turn: Press the same button. Simon then adds another light and sound to the sequence.
  • Your Turn: Repeat the entire sequence in the correct order. The sequence keeps growing, and you must remember the entire pattern.
  • If you successfully replicate the sequence, Simon continues to add to it as the level progresses. If you press the wrong button or mess up the order, the game ends.
  • Upon failure, to restart the game press any key on the keyboard.
  • NOTE : This game is made to be played on PC for now. Future updates incoming.
